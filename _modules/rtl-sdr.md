A voltage-controlled FM radio tuner for [VCVRack](https://vcvrack.com) supporting USB dongles via [librtlsdr](https://osmocom.org/projects/sdr/wiki/rtl-sdr). Based on [rtl_fm](http://kmkeen.com/rtl-demod-guide/)
